Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Overview

Free QR Generator ("we", "our", "the Service") is a client-side web application that lets you create QR codes directly in your browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit the content you enter (URLs, WiFi passwords, contact details, etc.) to any server. All QR code generation happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript.

Cookies & Analytics

With your consent, we may load privacy-friendly analytics (e.g. Plausible or Google Analytics) to understand usage patterns. Analytics does not receive your QR content. You can accept or decline via the cookie banner.

Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to save your theme preference (dark/light mode). This data stays on your device and is never sent to us.

Third-Party Services

The Service loads the following third-party resources from CDNs:

  • JavaScript libraries (QR rendering, export, color picker)
  • Google Fonts (Inter typeface)
  • Font Awesome (icons)

These providers may log your IP address when your browser requests their files. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Cookies

We do not set cookies. Third-party CDNs may use cookies according to their own policies.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
